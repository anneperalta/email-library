Frequently Asked Questions


What happens if I don’t verify my account?

You won't be able to process credit card payments and receive payouts to your bank account. If your account remains unverified for more than 30 days we may terminate your Payments service without further notice.


What’s required to complete verification?

You’ll need a scanned copy of a government issued photo identification, such as a drivers’ license or passport. If you have applied for your company, you may need to scan and upload your company registration documents.
