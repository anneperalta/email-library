Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if a payment fails?

You will receive email notifications regarding any failed payments. You can also view all failed payments under the Automated payments tab.

What happens when a card fails validation?

Automated payments will be unable to schedule payments if the card supplied fails validation. In this case, you will need to request an alternate card from your guest and manage any payments for the reservation manually.

Can I edit a rule?

A payment rule cannot be edited. You will need to archive the existing rule and create and activate a new rule with your preferred settings.
