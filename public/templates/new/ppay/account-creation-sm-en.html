In your dashboard, select the 'My Apps' tab in the top-right header and select 'Payments' from the dropdown menu. This will give you access to: a full list of your transactions through SiteMinder Pay; payout information for deposits into your bank account; access to your online payment terminal.

Looking for the easiest way to manage your account balance?

Auto Top Up automatically resolves negative balances on your account. It ensures you can receive payments from guests, receive payouts to your bank account and process refunds with no interruptions.
